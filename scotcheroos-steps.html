Steps

Butter a large cake pan on bottom and sides inside the pan. Set aside for later.

Measure all ingredients ( Use plastic wrap inside the measuring cup for peanut butter to reduce cleanup )

Place Rice Krispies into a large bowl

Pour cornsyrup in a microwave safe bowl and mix in brown sugar. Microwave for 1 minute then stir. Keep microwaving for increments of 1 minute and check until the sugar is dissolved in the syrup.

Stir in peanut butter into the sugary-corn syrup mixture. Add the mixture to the Rice Krispies in the bowl.

Stir until all of the cereal is coated. Use a gentle folding technique to prevent crumbling the cereal.

Once done, pour it into your buttered cake pan.

Use a rubber spatula or a large spoon to flatten the mixure out on the pan. The zig zag sliding of a spoon can help.

Pour the Chocolate Chips and Butterscotch into a microwave safe bowl and place it into the microwave. Keep microwaving for increments of 1 minute and check until they are into a melted chocolatey liquid.
